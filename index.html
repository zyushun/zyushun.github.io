About me

I'm a Ph.D student in School of Data Science at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, China. I'm honorable to be advised by Prof. Zhi-Quan Luo. I’m also very fortunate to work closely with Prof. Ruoyu Sun (UIUC). Previously, I did my undergraduate study in the Department of Mathematics at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). My research focuses on optimization and deep learning.
